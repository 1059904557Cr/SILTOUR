Misak University

The educational proposal of Ala Kusreik Ya Misak University of Special Official and Community Ethnic Character, is to work on the formative part that improves education and develops the expectations of the new generations to comply with the ancestral principles and foundations of the Misak and other Cultures. Therefore, formative processes are proposed that respond to the conception of training human and cultural talents, for the improvement of the Misak life and other Cultures, which can be expanded and deepened in the tasks, trades, practices, skills, knowledge, knowledge and traditions.
